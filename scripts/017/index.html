About This Calendar

This calendar is a "perpetual" or "fixed" calendar, designed for symmetry and simplicity. Its primary benefit is that any given date always falls on the same day of the week, forever.

Core Structure

  • **4 Seasons:** 91 days (13 weeks) each.
  • **12 Months:** A repeating 30, 30, 31 day pattern per season.
  • **364 Weekdays:** A perfect 52 weeks.
  • **1-2 Year Days:** Festive days outside the weekly cycle.

The Days of the Week

The 7-day week is constant. The year always begins on Solis.

  1. Solis (Sun's Day)
  2. Luna (Moon's Day)
  3. Terra (Earth's Day)
  4. Maris (Sea's Day)
  5. Ventus (Wind's Day)
  6. Silva (Forest's Day)
  7. Astra (Star's Day)
